## Game Overview

Guide Flora the Flamingo through ever-changing Florida landscapes. Tap to flap, avoid obstacles, collect power-ups, and see how far you can fly! Experience dynamic weather, day/night cycles, and the beauty of the Sunshine State.

## Features

### Core Gameplay
- **Intuitive Controls**: Tap or click to flap, release to glide
- **Momentum Physics**: Realistic yet forgiving flight mechanics
- **Increasing Difficulty**: The further you fly, the harder it gets

### Florida Elements
- **Dynamic Weather**: Sunny days, tropical rain, dramatic storms (via FloridaCore)
- **Day/Night Cycle**: Watch gorgeous sunsets and sunrises as you fly
- **Authentic Locations**: Beach, Everglades, Miami skyline, Space Coast
- **Local Wildlife**: Encounter pelicans, dolphins jumping, manatees below

## Controls

- **Desktop**: Mouse click or Spacebar to flap
- **Mobile**: Tap screen to flap
- **Menu**: Mouse/Touch to navigate

## Game Modes

- **Classic**: Standard endless flight
- **Weather Challenge**: Random weather changes
- **Sunset Mode**: Beautiful aesthetics, easier gameplay
- **Hurricane Mode**: Extreme difficulty with storm effects
